About Me

I'm currently a 5th year Ph.D. candidate in AI at Georgia State University who's interested in continual learning, multi-modality, scale, consciousness, and efficient contextual understanding beyond fixed-length inputs. I also hold a master's in Computer Science from GSU as well as a bachelor's in History from Duke University. Talk to me about all things AI, futbol, films, pizza, books, travel, friends, family, life.
